let boys cry

fuck toxic masculinity, nov 22, 2018

this is the name of the first collection

when i was little whenever i got emotional i would always get told, "boys don't cry" and I always thought that was stupid. i think it's lead to many issues particularly with toxic masculinity where men feel less inclined/comfortable with expressing their emotion, which feeds this toxic culture. not all, but a lot would get solved if we taught young boys and men that it's okay to show and share your emotions.

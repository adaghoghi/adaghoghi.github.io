'ridgewood?' is a photo essay i did for a class.
the assignment was to take photos of one neighborhood.
i chose ridgewood, as i had recently moved there, and wanted to take the opportunity to explore and understand more about the region.

this diptych is emblematic if the entire project for me.

i walked around the entire perimeter of ridgewood, throughout my time working on this project.
the picture on the left is the lower left corner of ridgewood, bordering bushwick.
this is where a huge portion of bushwick nightlife is located.
on the right is the upper right corner of ridgewood, neighboring glendale.
this photo was taken outside of stop and shop, a place that reminds me more of home than the city.
and considering the lack of transportation around this area, it might as well have been!

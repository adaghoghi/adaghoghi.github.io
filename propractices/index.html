ALVAND DAGHOGHI


Blog #1: Self-Pitch


My name is Alvand Daghoghi, and I’m currently a senior at NYU Tandon School of Engineering studying Integrated Digital Media (IDM).
Over the past year, I’ve taken classes in digital photography and I’m slowly transitioning into film photography.
My major is diverse in the design/media world, I’ve taken classes in web design, sound design/music production, graphic design, etc.
With that background, I moved into film production, working as a production designer.
Production design has taught me a lot about how to create, manipulate, and compose.
Whether that’s a fancy penthouse in soho, or the interior of a suburban home.
Working as a production designer changed the way I view photography.
I became much more understanding of the use of space, and how very little can affect so much in just a simple shot/frame.
It has also taught me how to navigate as a professional.
A production will always be a chaotic balance of casual/friendly and professional/diligent.
I’ve spent some time over the past year getting into, and now, experimenting with photography. I’ve made a few photo series, one of which called Ridgewood? is uploaded on medium.com. I’ve been trying to make it a habit to carry my camera with me at all times, even if I never take it out of my bag, having access to it allows me to ground myself in my work.
On top of my work in film and photography, I am the head of Public Relations for Delta Kappa Alpha, which is a cinematic fraternity at NYU. I’ve been directly in charge of the website for over a year, as my previous position was webmaster, and assist in planning and executing recruitment and various events around campus.
